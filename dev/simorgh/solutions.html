Simorgh software


Our Advanced Software for Automated and High-Precision Fracture Monitoring.

This new technology reduces the calculation time while improving the precision of damage-slip localizations, offering reliable fracture monitoring for diverse applications. Simorgh handles both continuous and trigger-based data types, and various data formats. 

We offer real-time and high precision monitoring of fractures from millimeters to kilometers size. Simorgh is versatile and adoptable for any solid materials, and supporting a wide range of common data formats. It enables integration with existing data acquisition systems and simplifies the transition from manual to automated monitoring process.

Simorgh hardware

Pre-amplifier with selective gain

We have designed pre-amplifiers with robust performance presenting fixed and selective gains from 0 to 100 dB. This instrument is suitable for both field and lab seismic-acoustic measurements. A fixed filter is applied to facilitate field interpretations.



→ Characteristics

• Bandwidth from 15 kHz to 2MHz

• Active bandpass filter with 750 kHz bandwidth frequency

• Out-of-band attenuation of 20db/decade

• Amplification from 0 to 15db

• Maximum output signal amplitude of +/-10V

• Easy amplification adjustment using built-in potentiometer

• Supply voltage in the 21.6VDC - 26.4VDC range

• Integrated reverse polarity protection

• Tested on piezoelectric sensors
