Missions & visions

Explore Simorgh's mission and vision to enhance safety, provide maintenance needs, and optimize resource management through fracture monitoring.


Our mission in providing automatic and precise fracture monitoring services is anchored in enhancing the safety of infrastructures and the well-being of people. By leveraging cutting-edge technology, we aim to revolutionize the way structural health is monitored, ensuring that any fractures or weaknesses are detected promptly and accurately. This proactive approach not only mitigates the risks of structural failures but also significantly boosts the efficiency of maintenance projects. Through our innovative solutions, we are committed to delivering peace of mind to infrastructure managers and stakeholders, ensuring that their projects are not only efficient and cost-effective but also uphold the highest standards of safety and reliability. This dedication to excellence reflects our deep commitment to safeguarding both our built environment and the communities that rely on it.

Our vision is to be a global leader in structural health monitoring, transforming how infrastructures are maintained and safeguarded. We aspire to create a world where structural failures are a thing of the past, achieved through our state-of-the-art, precise monitoring technologies. By continuously innovating and pushing the boundaries of what's possible, we aim to set follow the best practice standards in the industry, ensuring infrastructures are not just safe, but smartly and sustainably maintained. Ultimately, our goal is to foster absolute confidence in the safety and durability of the built environment, thereby enriching the quality of life for communities worldwide.
