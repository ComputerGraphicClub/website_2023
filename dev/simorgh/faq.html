Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common queries about Simorgh, fracture monitoring and our services.

01. What types of fractures can your service monitor?

Our service specializes in detecting and analyzing fractures pertinent to NDT (like surface and sub-surface cracks in materials), SHM (such as stress fractures in buildings and bridges), and microseismic-related fractures (like those occurring in geological structures and around mining operations).


02. How real-time is the fracture monitoring?

The fracture monitoring performed by our software is almost instantaneous, typically processing data in less than a second. However, slight delays may occur due to data transfer times from local machines to our servers.


03. Can your system integrate with existing infrastructure?

Yes, our system is designed for seamless integration with existing infrastructure, allowing for efficient deployment and operation.


04. How does your service ensure data accuracy and reliability?

The accuracy and reliability of our service are highly dependent on the quality of data received. By collaborating on network design and data acquisition, we can ensure proper measurements are taken, significantly enhancing the precision of our results.


05. What kind of support do you offer for troubleshooting and technical issues?

We provide comprehensive support, including technical assistance, troubleshooting guides, and a dedicated customer service team.


06. Is the data processed by your service secure?

Absolutely, data security is paramount in our service with robust encryption and strict adherence to privacy regulations.


07. How does your service contribute to the preventive maintenance of structures?

Our expert team evaluates the design of structures and applied forces to identify potential damage locations. This assessment aids in the efficient design of the monitoring network, enhancing preventive maintenance strategies by predicting future fracture risks.


08. What is the range of fracture sizes your service can monitor, and how does this cater to different industries?**

Our service can monitor fractures ranging from millimeters to kilometers, which enables us to effectively serve the diverse needs of the NDT, SHM, and Microseismic industrial communities. This broad range ensures comprehensive coverage for a variety of applications.


09. What is the cost structure of your service?

Our pricing is flexible and based on usage, scale, and specific client needs, ensuring cost-effectiveness for various budgets.


10. Can your service accommodate different data formats and types of signals?

Yes, our software is versatile in handling a variety of standard data formats, ranging from binary to ASCII, SEG-Y2, etc. Additionally, it is adept at processing both seismic and acoustic signals, ensuring broad compatibility and applicability.
