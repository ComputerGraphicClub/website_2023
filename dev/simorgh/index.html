This capability benefits maintenance and development in various sectors such as energy, infrastructure, and aerospace. This technological enhancement aids in elevating the quality of NDT practices.

Simorgh represents a notabe shift towards more streamlined and accurate monitoring in NDT. Simorgh offers a practical enhancement in Structural Health Monitoring (SHM), bringing refined accuracy and unprecedented real-time analytics to the field.

Its novel approach aids in more effectively identifying structural fractures and damages across scales from millimeters to tens of meters, making it a valuable tool for sectors like civil engineering and transportation. This technology delivers groundbreaking performance in precise, real-time SHM.

With Simorgh, SHM professionals can experience a shift towards more streamlined and informed monitoring processes. Microseismic monitoring

Simorgh revolutionizes microseismic and seismic monitoring by offering real-time and high-precision services, enabling decision-makers to take effective actions.

This technology facilitates the use of extensive sensor networks by enhancing data processing and interpretation, while reducing monitoring costs. Ideal for industries requiring real-time monitoring, such as mining, energy, and geotechnics, Simorgh significantly improves operational safety and efficiency.
