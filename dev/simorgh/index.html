Services

Non-Destructive Testing


Simorgh’s novel technology enables real-time and precise Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) of various material, while enhancing precise structural health assessments.

Its algorithm provides a reliable tool for more accurate fracture-damage detection across scales from micrometers to several meters. This capability benefits maintenance and development in various sectors such as energy, infrastructure, and aerospace. This technological enhancement aids in elevating the quality of NDT practices.

Simorgh represents a notabe shift towards more streamlined and accurate monitoring in NDT. It handles various data formats and adapts medium heterogeneity, which allows for improved maintenance procedures and increased safety. Simorgh stands as a valuable technology for the NDT community, aiming to strengthen the reliability and effectiveness of structural health evaluations.

Structural Health Monitoring


Simorgh offers a practical enhancement in Structural Health Monitoring (SHM), bringing refined accuracy and unprecedented real-time analytics to the field.

Its novel approach aids in more effectively identifying structural fractures and damages across scales from millimeters to tens of meters, making it a valuable tool for sectors like civil engineering and transportation. This technology delivers groundbreaking performance in precise, real-time SHM.

With Simorgh, SHM professionals can experience a shift towards more streamlined and informed monitoring processes. Its advanced capabilities and versatility allow for better maintenance strategies and reinforce safety measures for various structures. Simorgh represents a remarkable improvement in SHM, aiming to elevate industry practices in both efficiency and safety management.

Microseismic monitoring


Simorgh revolutionizes microseismic and seismic monitoring by offering real-time and high-precision services, enabling decision-makers to take effective actions.

This technology facilitates the use of extensive sensor networks by enhancing data processing and interpretation, while reducing monitoring costs. Ideal for industries requiring real-time monitoring, such as mining, energy, and geotechnics, Simorgh significantly improves operational safety and efficiency.

By supporting various data formats, recording types, and medium complexities, Simorgh stands out as a unique technology in microseismic monitoring. It offers an scalable solution and significantly improves safety and efficiency in seismic operations.
